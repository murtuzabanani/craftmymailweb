Frequently Asked Questions

We have answered the most neccessary answers here. If you have any other question feel free to contact us.

GENERAL

Do you offer refund policy?

Our main goal is your full satisfaction with our services. We offer partial and full refund depending on the stage of project.

We will provide a full refund if:

  • You are not satisfied with the final product, and you provide us with the clear reasons for your dissatisfaction.
  • The work on your order hadn't been started when the request for a refund was received.
  • The order could not be completed according to its specifications.

We respect you and your business, and want to provide you with the best service.

What is your turnaround time?
Usually we deliver email design with in 2 business days and a well coded email takes up to 3 days. While placing an order you will see the approximate delivery date. We may adjust the delivery date depending on project complexity. We start working only after the client's approval of delivery date and price. The turnaround time doesn't include time for revisions.
Do you work over weekends?
We don't work on weekends. To stay productive all over the week we need some time to relax and recharge ourself but still if you have something urgent to be done feel free to contact us and we'll figure out something for sure.
Do you offer revisions to designs and coding?
Yes, we offer unlimited revisions but its limited to bug fixes and minor tweaks. Any additions and sizable changes will be quoted separately.
Can I validate the design before placing an order?
Yes and thats what we prefer too. Just email us your deisgn files so we can inspect its compatibility and get back to you with free design consultaton.
I have more questions?
Sure we would love to answer them send your questions at hello@craftmymail.com or use contact form on our website. Also we have a live chat in case you need quick answers.
What is the best way to contact you?
We're developing our client area to have much smoother and centeralized infomration exchange with our clients. For now the best option is to contact us via email hello@craftmymail.com or use contact form on our website. Also we have a live chat facility in case you need quick answers.
Do you write content for emails?
We don't write content for emails. Although its fun but if you want us to suggest you some content alterations to your promotional emails just mention it while placing an order.

PLACING AN ORDER

How to place an order?
  1. Go to our pricing page and click the button Order Now.
  2. You will be redirected to a form that will ask you required questions to help us understand your needs clearly.
  3. Then you have to attach your design file or resources or images you want to include in your email design.
  4. Click on Place order and if you've filled everything then your order will be placed.
What payment methods do you accept?
All our prices are in USD. We accept Paypal and all major debit and credit cards.
When should I make the payment?
We require full payment before we start our work.
Do you offer any discount?
Yes we do offer bulk discounts. We also have discounted recurring plans for businesses and individual who wants our services regularly. Please contact us for more details.
Are there any discounts for NGO or non profit?
If you're an NGO or non profit let us know we respect your efforts and would like to contribute by offering a discount to our services.

EMAIL DESIGN & CODING

What do you require to design an email?
We need to know what the email is about and what are the contents. We will need logo, images, copywriting, text content, links and everything that you want to add in the email. If you don't have images and want us to use stock ones feel free to mention it as we do have a stock library, from where we can pick images for you. If you need us to buy images for your project then we can do that with additional charges.
What tools you use to design email?
We use the top design tools in the industry. Mainly we use Photoshop or Figma but we can also deliver you the file in Adobe XD, Adobe Illustrator and Sketch.
What do you require to convert my email to HTML?
We will need a layered design file built in Photoshop, Figma, Adobe XD, Adobe Illustrator or Sketch. Also need your instructions to understand if you have any special requirements.
Which file formats you accept for conversion?
Photoshop, Figma, Adobe XD, Adobe Illustrator or Sketch.
I have a flat image file for my email. Can you convert it to HTML email?
Yes. There may be an additional charge since we must turn them into a layered Photoshop file.
How do you test emails?
We use Litmus to test all our emails.
Do you code responsive email templates?
Yes. We have an extensive experience working with responsive email templates. You even don't have to provide a responsive email design version. We will make your template resize beautifully on any device, without any loss of quality!
I have an email template coded, but there are some issues. Can you fix it?
Sure, if it is possible. If not, we can recode it from scratch.
I want some email customization. Can you make the changes for me?
Sure. Get back to us with more details.
Do you provide screenshots from the tested email clients?
Absolutely! Just tell us if you need them!
Do you offer Mailchimp integrations?
Not yet. Currently we're not offering integration services but we will be offering it really soon. Stay tuned.
